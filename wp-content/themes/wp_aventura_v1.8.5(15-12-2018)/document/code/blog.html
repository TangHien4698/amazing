Blog No sidebar

How to create:

You can refer to Posts - Portfolios for more guidance of creating blog posts.

Step 1: Create set of your articles with categories

Step 2: Go to Theme options --> Blog --> Blog General > select no sidebar

Step 3: Go to Appearance --> Menus and add your blog category to menu.
